// Get Started

Launch B-OS in Seconds

No downloads. No installs. B-OS runs entirely in your browser. Just open the page and boot into your new desktop.

01

Open Your Browser

Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or any Chromium-based browser. B-OS is built for modern web standards.

02

Navigate to B-OS

Enter the B-OS URL in your address bar. The boot sequence begins automatically.

03

Boot Into Your Desktop

Watch the terminal boot sequence, then arrive at your desktop. Full OS simulation, zero friction.

04

Optional: Grant File Access

For the file manager module, grant browser permissions to access your local files. Fully optional.
